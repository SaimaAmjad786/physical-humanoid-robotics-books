## Who This Book Is For

- **Beginners**: Start from the basics with clear explanations and visual aids
- **Intermediate Learners**: Deepen your understanding through practical exercises
- **Educators**: Use as curriculum with structured learning objectives and assessments
- **Self-Learners**: Progress at your own pace with hands-on projects

## How to Use This Book

1. **Start with Chapter 1** if you're new to Physical AI
2. **Complete hands-on exercises** (marked with ðŸ”§) to solidify your understanding
3. **Use self-assessment sections** at the end of each lesson to check your progress

## Prerequisites

- Basic Python programming knowledge (variables, functions, loops, classes)
- Curiosity about robots and intelligent systems
- No hardware required - all exercises use Python simulations
